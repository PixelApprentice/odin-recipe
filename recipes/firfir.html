heard of Ethipian spicy food A.K.A 'firfir'

Firfir is a flavorful Ethiopian dish made from torn pieces of injera mixed with spicy berbere sauce and often combined with meat, vegetables, or lentils. It's a comforting, savory meal, typically eaten for breakfast or as a quick meal.

  • vegetables
  • lentils

Steps of preparing firfir

  1. Tear Injera: Tear injera into small pieces.
  2. Cook Spices: Saut√© onions, garlic, and berbere in oil.
  3. Add Sauce: Stir in water or broth to create a sauce.
  4. Mix Injera: Add torn injera to the sauce and stir until well coated.
  5. Add Optional Add-ins: Mix in cooked meat, lentils, or vegetables.
  6. Serve: Enjoy hot, often with a side of extra injera.
