Steps of cooking doro_wex

  1. Prepare Chicken: Clean the chicken, rub with lemon juice and salt, then set aside.
  2. In a pot, cook finely chopped onions in niter kibbeh until golden. Add garlic and ginger, cook briefly.
  3. Add Spices: Stir in berbere spice mix and tomato paste, cook for a few minutes to release flavors.
  4. Simmer: Add chicken, water, and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for about 45-60 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Boil Eggs: Hard-boil the eggs and add them to the stew in the last 10 minutes of cooking.
  6. Serve: Once cooked, serve with injera for a complete meal.
